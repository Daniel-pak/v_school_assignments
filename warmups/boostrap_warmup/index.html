As a long time student of coding, I participated in my own practice through CodeAcademy. I learned JavaScript, HTML and CSS, jQuery, Python through this method. I then used FreeCodeCamp to further my skills in HTML/CSS/JavaScript to create some functioning websites. After much research I decided on V School in Salt Lake City. After a vigorous admissions process, I joined the January cohort and flew out to Utah a week later. Now, week 2, I'm very invested in my learning and it is everything that I possible could have asked for. I love this program and everything it entails. Can't wait to learn so much more!
